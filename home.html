CHOSEN GEM
EMPIRE

Chosen Gem Empire (CGE) is a group of diverse talents that encourages young people to pursue their creativity Godly way. Using their talents to express the love of God, serve society with their ministry and gain income for their talents. Our members range from 6-10 years for kids and 11-25 years for the youth.
